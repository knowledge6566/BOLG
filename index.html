✦ About Bolgerr

A Blog Born From Curiosity

Bolgerr started in a small apartment in 2019 with a single idea: that great writing can change how we see the world. What began as a personal journal became one of the most-read independent blogs on the internet.

We write about technology, design, culture, science, and the fascinating collisions between them. Every piece is crafted with care — no clickbait, no filler, just ideas worth your time.

Our team of writers, researchers, and editors are united by a single belief: the best stories are the ones that make you think differently about something you thought you understood.

Our Journey

2019

Bolgerr Launches

Started as a personal blog with 3 articles and zero readers. The first post: "Why I Quit My Job to Write."

2020

First 10,000 Readers

A piece on remote work went viral. Within weeks, we hit 10K monthly readers and launched our newsletter.

2021

Team Expands

Brought on our first contributing writer and a dedicated editor. Published 100 articles this year alone.

2023

50,000 Subscribers

Our newsletter crossed 50K subscribers. Launched the Design and Science verticals.

2025

94K Monthly Readers

Bolgerr ranks among the top 500 independent blogs globally. Still independent, still ad-light.
